## 6. Server-Side Rendering Check

✅ **SSR is on.** `astro.config.mjs`:

```js
output: 'server',
adapter: process.argv.includes('dev') ? undefined : cloudflare({ imageService: 'passthrough' }),
```

Every public page is server-rendered HTML — ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot all see the fully-formed DOM without executing JS. This is the **single biggest structural win** and most platforms miss it.

Caveat: `output: 'server'` puts you on Cloudflare Workers, which means **edge caching is per-pop** — if SEO ranking depends on the cache hit rate, consider adding `Cache-Control` for public marketing pages (`/about/`, `/pricing/`, `/blog/*`).

## 15. What to Avoid

- ❌ Don't add `keywords` meta tag — Google ignores it and ChatGPT never reads it.
- ❌ Don't stuff FAQ markup on pages that don't have user-visible Q&A — Google will penalize.
- ❌ Don't add JSON-LD that contradicts visible content (e.g. `founder: "Jordan Miller"` with no matching visible text).
- ❌ Don't claim `Article` schema on the landing or about pages — only on real `/blog/posts/[slug]`.
